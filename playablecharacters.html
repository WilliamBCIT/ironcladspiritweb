Playable Characters:

Robot

Has good defensive stats, HP, SP, and Special Attack. Speed and attack are fairly low, so the support of additional party members will greatly help in battle.

Rowen

Surprisingly powerful, with great attack and speed, and good HP to boot! It's worth noting despite an incredible Special Attack stat, he has low SP and may be unable to use special attacks without recovery from time to time.

Oda

A character with lower HP, but has higher speed, SP, and Special Attack.

Gigi and Gerald (Duo)

This couple fights as a team! Despite being two different characters, in battle they work together with stats shared between them. Overall, they have fairly well-rounded stats, with the exception of low speed.

Brenda

Incredible defense from both ordinary and special attacks. Fantastic HP, too! However, she doesn't care to ever rush around, with a speed stat of zero. On the other hand, always taking action after a foe attacks helps with using some of her special attacks.

MVP

He's too cool and reckless to use a SP stat, so his special attacks use up some of his HP. Apart from that, his attack stats and speed are huge. Defense stats are a bit shaky, though.
